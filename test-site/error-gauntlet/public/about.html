About NimbusAI

We're on a mission to make writing effortless for everyone.

Our Story

NimbusAI started in 2024 when our founders — a journalist, a developer, and a linguist — realized that AI could help writers focus on ideas instead of mechanics. We built the first prototype in a weekend hackathon and haven't stopped since. Read our full founding story.

Our Mission

We believe everyone has stories worth telling. NimbusAI removes the friction between thought and written word, helping professionals and creatives alike produce their best work faster.
